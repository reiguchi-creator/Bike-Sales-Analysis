## Bike Sales Analysis Dashboard  

# Project Overview  
This project provides an in-depth analysis of Bike Sales performance across multiple demographics, regions, and product categories.
Using an Excel spreadsheet as the data source and Tableau for visualization, the dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights, enabling data-driven decision-making.

# Objective  
To create an interactive Tableau dashboard that visualizes sales performance, highlights trends across products and regions, and supports strategic business analysis.  

# Key Insights  
- 2015 Revenue & Profit: $20M revenue (+28% YoY) with $7.5M profit
- Top Categories: Bikes dominate revenue; Accessories and Clothing contribute smaller portions
- Top Products: Helmets, water bottles, tires, and essential bike components
- Customer Demographics: Young adults and adults (25–64 years) drive purchases
- Geographic Reach: Sales concentrated in major markets including the US, UK, Germany, Canada, France, and Australia

# Tools & Skills Demonstrated  
- **Tableau** – Interactive dashboards, visualizations, filters, parameters, and calculated measures 
- **Analytical Storytelling** – Translating complex datasets into clear, actionable narratives
- **Data Analysis** - Identifying patterns and trends to support business decision-making
